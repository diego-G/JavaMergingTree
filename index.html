Merging tree excersice

Below, there are two files with data about two trees:

  • File1 --> slash separated paths A/B/C : 1 A/B : 1 A1/B1/C : 1

  • File2 --> dot or slash separated paths A.C : 1 A.B : 1 A.B.D : 3 A.B.C : 1 A2/B/C : 4

Expected result : A : 8 A/B : 7 A/B/C : 2 A/B/D : 3 A/C : 1 A1 : 1 A1/B1 : 1 A1/B1/C : 1 A2 : 4 A2/B : 4 A2/B/C : 4

It is needed to merge the two trees and generate a full layout of the tree.
